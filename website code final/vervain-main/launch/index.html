TheMindfullReaders

The MindfullReaders is a non-profit organization for anyone who wants to improve their public speaking, communication skills, and become more fluent in English. Our goal is to provide a safe, supportive, and judgment-free space where people can practice speaking, share ideas, and learn how to express themselves with confidence. If you're someone who wants to speak more clearly, connect with others better, and look at the world with new perspectives—we're here for you. The book discussions we are having aren’t just presentations—they’re open conversations. Anyone can respond, share a different opinion, or add to the discussion. This is not just an open mic, it's a discussion room—where we practice public speaking, learn how to communicate respectfully, and grow together.

Our Vision

we aim to build a supportive community where young minds grow through reading, open discussions, and shared insights. We envision empowering individuals with confidence, communication skills, and awareness to connect literature with real-world change.

Founder's Voice

So, when I was kid who used to stammers for most of his life earlier. Everyone used to make used to make fun of me for the way I speak and that made me not to talk to anyone but one day I decided okay I will stop this, whatever is the bug in me I’ll fix it. Then I taken professionals advice on this matter, countless yt videos and google research on this practices all alone with myself and the mirror, years of practice and when I actually became able to control it a bit then I understood the problem which was all I need is speak and talk with people. Stammering is not actually a biological problem it’s just a condition when a person is nervous and anxious he develops a habit of taking pause while speaking and that can only be solved by talking to people confidently but there was no place where I can practiced it because whenever I speak everyone just laugh at me. My friends, and even sometimes my family which made me realize that are out there so many people like who are suffering with same, which has given me the idea of doing something for them, people who are suffering with the problem just like me but are not able to talk about it because this is something that mainly no one cares about that much. But it a matter of consideration because only a person who stammers know how it feel. So that’s why I create this club. A safe & supportive place with no judgment of anything with no age restrictions or anything. you want to talk and speak to people about your thoughts, ideas or beliefs and wants to share your perspective of seeing the world without the fear of being a joke in front of everyone. we are here for you!
